Wizard Composition I - Game Jam

Project Information

  • Category: Game Design, Visual Novel
  • Project URL: Play Wizard Composition I

A unique wizard 'dating' sim visual novel. Players choose a wizard partner with whom they'll work on a thesis paper, a journey that spans years. Each wizard has their own quirks and strengths, making the choice of partner crucial for both the academic and personal paths the player will navigate. Choose wisely, as this partnership will shape the story and experience.
